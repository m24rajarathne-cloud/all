Our Story

Founded in 2020, Go Cart started with a simple mission: to make fresh, quality groceries accessible to everyone. What began as a small local delivery service has grown into one of the most trusted online supermarkets.

We partner directly with local farmers and premium suppliers to ensure that every product meets our high standards. From farm-fresh vegetables to artisanal baked goods, we bring the best to your table.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Go Cart

How does Go Cart delivery work?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and checkout. Our delivery team will pick and pack your order, then deliver it to your doorstep within your chosen time slot. You can track your order in real-time through our app.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery is FREE for all orders over $50. For orders below $50, a flat delivery fee of $4.99 applies. Express delivery (within 2 hours) is available for an additional $9.99.

How do I return a product?

If you are not satisfied with a product, you can request a return within 24 hours of delivery. Simply go to your order history, select the item, and click "Return". Our team will arrange a pickup and process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Are your products organic?

We offer a wide range of organic products clearly labeled with our organic badge. All organic items are certified and sourced from trusted organic farms. You can filter products by "Organic" in our categories page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, Google Pay, and Cash on Delivery. All online payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

How do I use coupon codes?

During checkout, you will find a coupon code field. Enter your code and click "Apply". The discount will be automatically applied to your order total. Note that some coupons may have minimum order requirements.
